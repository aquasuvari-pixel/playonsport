ℹ️ How PlayOnSport Works

🥇

1. Create Your Sports Profile

Sign up by entering your volleyball level and preferred playing position. This helps us ensure balanced and competitive matches.

📅

2. Pick an Event

Browse through the available upcoming volleyball events organized under ADS Sports Club quality in London. Find the time and location that fits you best.

👥

3. The 12-Player Group System

Each event has a strict maximum capacity of 12 players (6v6). Once the 12th spot is taken, the group automatically closes, and the match is officially confirmed.

💳

4. Confirm and Play

Secure your spot via a simple bank transfer as directed by the administration. Show up at the court, meet your team, and enjoy premium amateur sports magic!
